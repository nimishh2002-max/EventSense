STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES (SOP) - EVENT SAFETY & COMPLIANCE
Revision: 2024-Q1

SECTION 1: FIRE SAFETY & CROWD CONTROL
1.1 Capacity Limits: Events exceeding 50 attendees must have at least 2 distinct fire exits clearly marked.
1.2 Aisle Width: For indoor venues with seated arrangements, a clear 4-foot aisle width must be maintained at all times.
1.3 Pyrotechnics: No open flames, candles, or pyrotechnics are allowed without a signed Fire Marshal approval (Form FM-99) submitted 14 days in advance.
1.4 Emergency Announcements: The event host must read the standard emergency exit script at the start of any event with >100 people.

SECTION 2: FOOD & ALCOHOL
2.1 Alcohol Service: Alcohol service is strictly regulated. It requires a licensed third-party vendor with insurance. Students or faculty are not permitted to serve alcohol directly.
2.2 Food Safety: Any event serving food to >100 people must have a Certified Food Safety Manager on-site for the duration of the event.
2.3 Hydration: Free water stations must be accessible for all events lasting longer than 2 hours.

SECTION 3: OUTDOOR VENUE RULES
3.1 Noise Ordinances: Outdoor amplified sound is prohibited after 10:00 PM on weekdays (Sun-Thu) and 11:00 PM on weekends (Fri-Sat).
3.2 Lawn Protection: Events on the 'South Lawn' or 'Main Quad' involving heavy equipment (stages, generators) require ground protection mats.
3.3 Weather Contingency: All outdoor events must have a documented "Rain Plan" (indoor backup booking) if the chance of precipitation exceeds 30%.
